FASHION EVENT

ABOUT EVENT

Fashion Week is a bi-annual event held in Mumbai in March and Delhi in October. It's a major platform for designers, brands, and industry stakeholders to showcase their collections and redefine fashion2. Here are some key details: History: FW started in 1999 and has been held over 25 times. Frequency: It takes place twice a year. Locations: The Summer-Resort show is held in Mumbai, while the Winter-Festive show is held in Delhi. Participants: Over 100 designers, including established names like Manish Malhotra, Rohit Bal, and emerging talents. Showcases: The event features a mix of established and emerging designers, luxury brands, and international models. Purpose: To integrate India into the global fashion world and promote sustainability in fashion. FW has launched the careers of many designers and has seen participation from Indian film stars and international models. It's a significant event in the Indian fashion calendar.

SERVICES

Fashion Week offers a variety of services and opportunities for designers, brands, and industry professionals. Here are some key services provided: Designer Showcases: Designers get a platform to present their collections to a global audience, including buyers, media, and fashion enthusiasts. Networking Opportunities: The event facilitates networking with industry professionals, buyers, and media, helping designers and brands build valuable connections. Media Coverage: Lakme Fashion Week attracts significant media attention, providing extensive coverage and exposure for participants. Sustainability Initiatives: The event promotes sustainable fashion through various programs and challenges, encouraging designers to adopt eco-friendly practices. Emerging Talent Programs: Programs like the GenNext Designer Program and the Circular Design Challenge support and mentor emerging designers. Fashion and Lifestyle Experiences: The event curates unique fashion and lifestyle experiences, including fashion shows, exhibitions, and interactive sessions.
